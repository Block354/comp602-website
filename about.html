About Tunnel Stone

Tunnel Stone has been a well established family business since 1985. Starting as a small business, Tunnel Stone has grown to one of the top nation-wide building material and hardware suppliers. We have decades of experience and knowledge and are 100% commited to our partners and customers.

We sell over 37,000 product lines from tools to electrical and timber to plumbing. We strive to source the best quality products for the building and hardware industry.
